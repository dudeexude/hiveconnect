About HiveConnect

HiveConnect: Connecting HPTU & Beyond is a networking platform that connects students and alumni to build a stronger academic and professional community. It provides access to career counselling, internships, job opportunities, and mentorship, while also enabling alumni connections through branch and skill-based filters. The system manages upcoming events with clear participation criteria, ensuring students never miss important workshops or meets. By uniting counselling, internships, career growth, and networking in one place, HiveConnect empowers students and alumni to collaborate, learn, and grow together.
